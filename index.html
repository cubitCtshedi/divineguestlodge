Frequently Asked

Questions About Divine Guest Lodge

Everything business travellers, contractors, and leisure visitors need to know about staying at our Rustenburg guest house.

Where is Divine Guest Lodge located in Rustenburg?
Divine Guest Lodge is at 12 Keurtjie Avenue, Rustenburg, 0299, in the North West Province of South Africa. We are approximately 5 minutes' drive from Rustenburg CBD, 7 minutes from Waterfall Mall, and 45 minutes from Sun City. The N4 and R565 highways are easily accessible from our location.
How much does it cost to stay at Divine Guest Lodge?
We have 20 rooms across two room types: the Deluxe Room (Bed Only) at R1,000 per night with a double bed, and the Family Room at R1,300 per night with two double beds (sleeps up to four guests). All prices include WiFi, parking, daily housekeeping, and CCTV. No hidden fees.
What types of rooms are available?
We offer two en-suite room types across our 20 rooms: Deluxe Room (double bed, R1,000/night, Bed Only — ideal for solo travellers and couples) and Family Room (two double beds, R1,300/night — sleeps up to four guests). All rooms include private bathrooms, smart TVs, WiFi, and daily housekeeping.
Is Divine Guest Lodge safe and secure?
Yes. We have perimeter fencing, and full CCTV surveillance. Off-street parking is included and secured. We're particularly trusted by solo travellers, female visitors, and contractors with valuable equipment. Many of our returning visitors cite security as their top reason for coming back.
Is WiFi included with my stay?
Yes — uncapped, high-speed WiFi is included with every booking. It's reliable enough for video calls, streaming, and remote work. Many of our business travellers work from the lodge during the day.
Do you offer long-term or weekly stays?
Absolutely. We have weekly and monthly rates for mining contractors and professionals on extended assignments in the platinum belt. The Deluxe Room is our most popular long-term choice. Contact us directly on +27 14 000 0000 or info@divineguestlodge.co.za for negotiated long-stay pricing.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is from 14:00 (2:00 PM) and check-out is by 10:00 (10:00 AM). Early check-in and late check-out can sometimes be arranged on request — just contact reception in advance.
How do I book a room?
You can book directly via any "Book Now" or "Reserve" button on this page (which takes you to Booking.com), call us on +27 14 000 0000, or email info@divineguestlodge.co.za. Free cancellation is available on most rates.
Is Divine Guest Lodge close to Sun City and Pilanesberg?
Yes — Sun City is approximately 45 minutes' drive and Pilanesberg National Park is around 50 minutes away. Many leisure travellers use Divine Guest Lodge as an affordable, secure base while exploring the area, especially given how much cheaper it is than staying inside Sun City itself.
